It's Just a Game, why you Have to be Mad?

Medium: Twine interactive fiction

Dimensions: Variable (browser-based)

Year: 2024

Play Interactive Story

About this Work

This interactive narrative invites players into the emotional turbulence of gaming and daily routines. Built using Twine, the story branches based on player choices, exploring isolation, frustration, and the peculiar ways in which virtual and real-world stressors overlap. The piece highlights how game culture can impact our moods and the subtle strangeness of the mundane.

Through branching narrative paths, players experience the psychological weight of gaming culture and its intersection with daily life. The interactive format allows for multiple perspectives on the same emotional journey, revealing how individual choices shape our understanding of frustration and escape.
