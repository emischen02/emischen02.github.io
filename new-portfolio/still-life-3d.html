Download Blender File

About this Work

This rendered still life combines form, material, and lighting study in a 3D graphics environment. Marble, ceramic, and glass textures are simulated digitally, with a glowing interior light and a geometric background. The piece invites viewers to contemplate the relationship between traditional artistic motifs and digital rendering.

Through careful attention to material properties and lighting, this 3D study explores how digital tools can recreate and reinterpret classical still life traditions. The interplay between hard and soft surfaces, transparency and opacity, creates a contemporary take on timeless artistic themes.
