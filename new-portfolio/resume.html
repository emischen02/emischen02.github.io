Profile

My portfolio showcases my work in interactive media and creative coding, particularly my projects in Max. I want to create a gallery where I can share my experience and progress with family, friends, future artists, and potential collaborators. While I am not currently looking for a job, I want my portfolio to be a record of my work that I can later use for future job opportunities. My strengths lie in blending technology with art to create engaging music videos and 3d model experiences. My work often explores interactivity and perception and I want my portfolio to reflect these themes. My creative vision requires visitors to experience my work in person rather than static images, which not only showcases my work as intended but results in a much more exciting experience for the viewers.
