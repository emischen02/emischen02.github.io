Art 75 Group Project – Glitch and Connectivity

Medium: Video art, digital media collaboration

Dimensions: 1080 x 720 px (single frame, video duration: 4:50)

Year: 2024

View Video

About this Work

This collaborative video explores concepts of connection, distortion, and digital presence. Overlaying smartphone interaction with glitch art effects, the group project examines how technology shapes and warps our perception of everyday communications. Vivid colors and fragmented imagery represent both the promise and breakdown of human connection in the digital age.

The piece investigates the intersection of technology and human interaction, using glitch aesthetics to reveal the underlying tensions in digital communication. Through collaborative creation, we explored how individual perspectives merge and diverge in the digital realm.
