About Me:

To learn more about my work history, check out my Portfolio. Thanks for stopping by!

Artist's Statement:

My work explores the connections between emotion, environment, and imagination through a range of media including drawing, digital art, mixed media, and photography. I see art as a language that expresses what words often cannot—transforming everyday moments and personal experiences into visual stories that invite reflection and curiosity. What makes my work unique is my willingness to experiment with color, texture, and light to balance realism and abstraction, structure and spontaneity. Guided by intuition, I allow each piece to evolve naturally, embracing accidents and discoveries along the way. Through my art, I aim to evoke emotion, inspire thought, and remind viewers of the quiet beauty that exists in ordinary moments.
